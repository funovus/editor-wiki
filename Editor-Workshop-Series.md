## Workshop Series 2020

We've put together a video series from the editor workshops we ran in 2020 that you may find useful. The editor UI/UX has changed a bit from when these videos were recorded, but the workflow and design principles are generally the same as they are today. These workshops cover topics ranging from editor tips and tricks to UI demos to gameplay and design polish. Check the playlist [here](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLe0Qf5eG3k7ow1ef7BGKQw4DI5O0ScU21).

You can grab the map files for these workshops below (though map files for #4 and #5 are sadly absent).
